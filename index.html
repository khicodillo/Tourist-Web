About Us

Welcome to Pateros — the Balut Capital of the Philippines!

Nestled in the heart of Metro Manila, Pateros is a small yet vibrant municipality known for its rich cultural heritage, warm-hearted locals, and time-honored traditions. As the only remaining municipality in the region, Pateros proudly preserves its unique identity while embracing modern growth.

Our town is best known for its world-famous balut (boiled fertilized duck egg), but there's so much more to discover — from our centuries-old churches and lively festivals to peaceful riverside walks and thriving local industries.

This website was created to showcase the beauty, history, and soul of Pateros. Whether you're a curious traveler, a returning visitor, or someone looking to experience authentic Filipino culture, we're here to guide you on a journey filled with unforgettable experiences.

Come explore Pateros — where tradition lives on and every moment feels like coming home.
